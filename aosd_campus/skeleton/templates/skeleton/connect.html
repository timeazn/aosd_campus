JOIN A BIBLE STUDY

Whether you're curious about Christianity or searching for answers to real doubts, we believe that studying the Bible with others in a personal setting is one of the most effective ways to grow in your faith.

Our Bible studies are engaging, personalized, and flexible with wherever you're at. We believe that conversations, more than information, changes lives. We welcome all questions, all people, and all backgrounds to our Bible studies--

So if you want to share a cup of coffee with us or meet on your college campus to chat, feel free to leave your contact information here and we will get back to you as soon as we can. We look forward to connecting with you!
