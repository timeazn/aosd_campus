WE ARE AO SAN DIEGO

We are the San Diego Chapter of a nation-wide campus ministry

Alpha Omega is a college group of the San Diego Church of Christ. We're comprised of students from colleges all over the county, and we're dedicated to knowing God deeply and making God known on our campuses.

Our focus is to apply the Bible to our lives, build deep friendships, deep convictions and have a lot of fun. We strive to know Christ personally, and help others do the same everywhere we go.

OUR VALUES

Bible-Based Teaching

"For the word of God is living and active"

We strive to live according to God's word, using the teachings of Jesus in our everyday lives.

One Another

"Above all, love each other deeply"

The relationships that we have with one another serve as a springboard to advance our faith.

The Great Commission

"Therefore go and make disciples of all nations"

Our goal is to live out the mission of Jesus as he has called us to go and show others this way of life.
